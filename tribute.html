Life

Dedan Kimathi Waciuri was born in 31st October 1920
His Father valued education and therefore Kimathi was sent to school where his skills in writing and speech were noted
Upon taking the oath of the Mau Mau, in 1951 he joined the Forty group as branch secretary and he presided over oath taking
On 21st October 1956 he was captured by the british
18th February 1957 marked the end of life as he was excecuted by the brits by hanging
He was buried in an unmarked grave which was discovered in 2019 by the Kimathi foundation
